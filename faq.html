How do I place an order?

Browse our catalog, select the PC or components you want, and click “Add to Cart.” When you're ready, go to your cart and follow the checkout steps. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.

Can I customize my PC before buying?

Yes! Most of our systems can be customized. Choose your processor, graphics card, RAM, storage, and more using the “Customize” button on the product page.

Do you sell parts separately (GPU, CPU, etc.)?

Absolutely. You can purchase individual components under the “Parts” section.

How do I know if a component is compatible?

Our customization tool automatically checks compatibility between components. You can also contact our support team for advice before you buy.

Do your PCs come with a warranty??

Yes. All PCs come with a 1-year standard warranty, covering parts and labor. You can extend it up to 3 years at checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and in some regions, financing through Klarna or Affirm.

My PC won't boot. What should I do?

  • Check that all cables are securely connected.

  • Ensure your monitor is powered on and connected to the GPU (not motherboard).

  • Try a different outlet or power cable.

  • If it still doesn't start, contact our support team.

The PC is running slow — what can I do?

  • Close unnecessary background apps.

  • Make sure Windows and drivers are up to date.

  • Check for malware with Windows Defender.

  • Contact us if the issue persists — a faulty component may be covered under warranty.

How long does delivery take?

  • Pre-built PCs: Usually ship within 1-3 business days.

  • Custom builds: Typically ship within 7-10 business days (depending on configuration).

  • Parts: Usually 2-5 business days.

What if my PC arrives damaged or defective?

Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or repair at no additional cost.
